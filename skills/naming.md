# Skill — naming

- Use full English names that distinguish domain roles.
- Let namespace provide inherited context.
- Preserve external names only at their boundary.
- Use literal design vocabulary rather than analogy in design names, comments, and docs: replace `mouth` with `textual interface` or `form`, `organs` with `nametree and structuretree`, `spine` with `core invariant` or `core pathway`, `door` with `entry point`, and `currency` with `value type`; retain `Textual` as the existing interface name.
